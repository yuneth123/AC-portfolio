About me

Hi, I'm Yuneth Samarasinghe, a passionate front-end developer with a keen eye for design and functionality. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Vue.js, I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly web interfaces. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful designs and clean, efficient code.

My goal is to craft websites and web applications that not only look great but also provide an intuitive and seamless user experience. I stay up-to-date with the latest front-end technologies, trends, and best practices to ensure the work I deliver is always modern and cutting-edge.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the latest in web design, contributing to open-source projects, or refining my skills to take on new challenges.
